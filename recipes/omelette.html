Omelette Recipe

Description

A well-made omelet is the icing on the cake for breakfast. Although the ingredients may vary, if you have 3 or 4 eggs to prepare a fluffy omelet recipe, that's it! Of course, you can make a plain omelet with 1 or 2 eggs, if you wish, by reducing the amount of butter and olive oil. The well-beaten eggs are placed in the pan and cooked in a short time. It should be neither watery nor too solid.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions

  1. For the omelet, put 4 eggs in a deep mixing bowl.
  2. Combine half a teaspoon of salt and pepper. Beat well with a whisk from bottom to top until air bubbles form.
  3. Heat your pan and heat 1 tablespoon each of butter and olive oil.
  4. Then pour the egg mixture into the pan. Reduce the heat of your stove and cook until the bottom part is browned.
  5. When the bottom part of the omelet is browned and the top part is slightly watery, tilt your pan first and fold the omelet in half.
  6. Cook for another 1-2 minutes and remove from the stove.
  7. Serve the omelet with breakfast items before it cools down. Enjoy!
