Menemen Recipe

Description

Especially when there is menemen on the table for breakfast on the weekend, that table becomes cheerful and beautiful. You can't get enough of dipping bread! Although menemen is known as a bachelor's or student food, it can be easily prepared and consumed at any meal of the day due to the high nutritional value it contains such as tomatoes, peppers, onions and eggs. You can't get enough of its taste! Let's prepare a delicious menemen for breakfast today that you can enjoy at any time of the day. Here is the recipe for menemen with tomatoes, peppers and onions! Of course, you can also prepare it without onions.

Ingredients

  • 4 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 onion
  • 4 green peppers
  • 1 red pepper
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 1 sprig of spring onion
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper

Directions

  1. Put the olive oil in a non-stick pan and fry the finely chopped onion until it turns pink.
  2. Then add the green peppers and bell peppers and continue to fry.
  3. When the peppers start to wilt, add the diced tomatoes.
  4. Cover the lid and cook until the tomatoes soften.
  5. About 10 minutes will be enough.
  6. Finally, add the finely chopped spring onion.
  7. When the onions turn pale, break the eggs and add the spices and mix.
  8. Stir until the eggs are cooked, then turn off the stove and serve the onion menemen hot.
  9. Enjoy!
